# Automated Budget Decision Infrastructure

## The Reality

Every marketing organization eventually needs a reliable way to decide how budget should be allocated across channels under uncertainty.

Today, this responsibility sits with analysts and managers, supported by dashboards that report performance but do not make decisions.
As data becomes noisier, channels fragment, and costs rise, heuristic-based judgment becomes increasingly fragile.

Most teams eventually attempt to build internal decision logic.
Very few finish.

---

## The Shift

This system reframes budget allocation as infrastructure, not judgment.

Instead of asking analysts to interpret metrics and make ad hoc calls, it provides:
- explicit recommendations (increase, monitor, reduce)
- uncertainty-aware reasoning
- consistent behavior across accounts and conditions

Decisions become repeatable, conservative, and scalable.
